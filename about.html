Ekatra: Collection of resources to guide you to success


Our site aims at raisng awareness about the importance of STEM education by providing articles from different established firms at one place.

The goal is to give girls who live in remote areas and have limited access to online resources access to all the information they require in one location, so that they can use them to make an informed decision thus saving them time that they could use to focus on their studies.

The site also has a planner which can be used to keep track of the upcoming events or taking notes.
